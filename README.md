# Grass Roots

Grass Roots is a platform that connects teachers, landowners, and students through
hands-on learning experiences in the real world.

Anyone with a valuable skill — whether it's permaculture, natural building,
woodworking, beekeeping, regenerative farming, software development, art, music, or
something completely different — can launch a **teacher residency** at a host site.
Living landscapes in need of specific skills are matched to teachers who deliver that
work through a course, program, or workshop, and students learn by doing alongside them.

## How it works

- **Landowners** provide the space and benefit from the projects and community that
  form around them.
- **Teachers** earn income while sharing their knowledge.
- **Students** gain practical skills through immersive, project-based learning.

## Vision

The long-term vision is to build a global network of learning communities where people
can travel, teach, learn, and collaborate — creating meaningful projects that leave a
lasting impact on both the land and the people involved.
